Company Overview

About CMR Surgical

CMR Surgical is a global medical technology company dedicated to transforming surgery through innovative robotic technology. Founded with the mission to make robotic-assisted surgery accessible to hospitals and surgeons worldwide, the company has developed the Versius Surgical System, a next-generation robotic platform designed for versatility, portability, and ease of use.

The Versius Surgical System

The Versius Surgical System represents a breakthrough in surgical robotics, featuring a modular, portable design that can be easily integrated into existing operating rooms without major infrastructure changes. The system is FDA-authorized for cholecystectomy procedures in adult patients 22 years of age and older.
