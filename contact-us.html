Frequently Asked Questions

How can I learn more about the Versius system?

You can explore our website, request a demonstration, or contact our clinical team for detailed information about the Versius Surgical System's capabilities and benefits.

What training is required to use Versius?

All surgeons must complete our comprehensive training program, which includes didactic learning, simulation training, and proctored clinical cases. Our training team provides ongoing support throughout the certification process.

Is technical support available 24/7?

Yes, we provide round-the-clock technical support for all Versius system users. Our expert technical team is available to assist with any system-related questions or issues.

How do I request a product demonstration?

Contact us through this form or reach out to your regional representative to schedule a demonstration of the Versius Surgical System at your facility or at one of our training centers.
