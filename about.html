About CMR Surgical

Transforming surgery. For good.

Our Mission

CMR Surgical is dedicated to transforming surgery through innovative robotic technology. We believe that advanced surgical robotics should be accessible to hospitals and surgeons worldwide, enabling better patient outcomes and more efficient healthcare delivery.

Our Vision

We envision a future where robotic-assisted surgery is the standard of care, providing surgeons with enhanced precision, dexterity, and control while maintaining the human touch that is essential to patient care.

The Versius Advantage

Our flagship product, the Versius Surgical System, represents a new generation of surgical robotics. Designed with modularity, portability, and ease of use in mind, Versius enables hospitals to integrate robotic surgery into their existing workflows without major infrastructure changes.

Innovation and Research

At CMR Surgical, we are committed to continuous innovation. Our research and development teams work tirelessly to advance the field of robotic surgery, collaborating with leading surgeons and medical institutions to ensure our technology meets the evolving needs of healthcare providers.

Global Impact

Since our founding, we have been focused on making a global impact in healthcare. Our technology is designed to be accessible and adaptable, enabling hospitals of all sizes to benefit from the advantages of robotic-assisted surgery.
