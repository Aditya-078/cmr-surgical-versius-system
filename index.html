Meet Versius

A surgical robot developed and engineered for versatility and adaptability, driven by a digital ecosystem embedded at its core. Versius has the potential to empower hospitals and surgeons to transform surgery.

Versius is a Robotic-Assisted Surgical Device intended to assist in the precise and accurate control of Versius Surgical endoscopic instruments and is indicated in the U.S. for adult patients 22 years of age and older, eligible for soft tissue minimal access surgery, for cholecystectomy. For full product and safety information, please refer to Important Safety Information.

How Versius is designed to empower you:

Designed around the human arm

The Versius Surgical System is a small, modular and versatile surgical robotic system used to perform cholecystectomy via soft tissue minimal access surgery. It is designed to biomimic the human arm, giving surgeons the choice of optimized port placement alongside, 3D HD vision, a 710° range of motion, and the dexterity, precision, and accuracy of small fully-wristed instruments.

Seamless integration for you and your team

Versius is being designed with adaptability in mind, allowing seamless movement between operating rooms through its portable, modular design. Its compact footprint fits even the smallest ORs, while its collaborative design can help ensure continuous patient access. Versius has the potential to effortlessly to meet your surgical needs, enhancing flexibility and efficiency in virtually any care setting.

Supporting surgeons with a comprehensive training pathway

Alongside multiple channels, such as the Versius Trainer, Virtual Reality, our online learning platform and practical training and assessment, our friendly, expert teams of professional skills trainers will be able to provide ongoing support throughout every step of the comprehensive metrics-led Versius training pathway.

How is Versius designed for easy adoption?

With a compact, modular, and portable design Versius has the potential to fit into virtually any operating room, allowing it to be used with your existing workflow without the requirement to make changes to the hospital or care setting. Through flexible port placement, Versius allows you to build on your existing laparoscopic knowledge, with familiar port placement, rapidly getting surgeons to proficiency with surgical robotics.
