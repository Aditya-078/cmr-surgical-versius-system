CMR Surgical secures more than $200M to accelerate growth

Published: April 4, 2025

CMR Surgical, the developer of the next-generation Versius Surgical System, has successfully closed a financing round of more than $200 million. The round was backed by existing investors and welcomed new investor Trinity, demonstrating strong confidence in the company's vision and technology.

This significant investment will enable CMR Surgical to accelerate the global expansion of the Versius Surgical System, particularly following the recent FDA marketing authorization for cholecystectomy procedures in the United States.

"This funding represents a major milestone for CMR Surgical and validates our approach to making robotic surgery more accessible," said the company's leadership team. "We're excited to use this investment to bring the benefits of the Versius system to more hospitals and patients worldwide."

The Versius Surgical System's modular, portable design sets it apart in the robotic surgery market, allowing hospitals to integrate advanced surgical robotics without major infrastructure changes.

CMR Surgical receives U.S. FDA Marketing Authorization for next-generation Versius Surgical System

Published: October 14, 2024

The U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) has granted CMR Surgical marketing authorization for the Versius Surgical System (Versius) for use in cholecystectomy procedures. This landmark approval paves the way for the commercial launch of Versius in the United States market.

The Versius Surgical System is indicated for adult patients 22 years of age and older who are eligible for soft tissue minimal access surgery, specifically for cholecystectomy procedures. The system's unique design mimics the human arm, providing surgeons with enhanced dexterity and precision.

  • 3D HD vision system for superior visualization
  • Fully-wristed instruments for precise control
  • Modular, portable design for flexible OR integration

This authorization represents years of rigorous testing and clinical validation, demonstrating the safety and efficacy of the Versius system in surgical applications.

CMR Surgical Strengthens Senior Team

Published: September 3, 2024

CMR Surgical has announced key appointments to its senior leadership team as the company prepares for expanded global operations and U.S. market entry.

Markus Bauman has been appointed as Chief Legal & Business Affairs Officer and will join CMR's executive leadership team. Bauman brings extensive experience in healthcare technology and regulatory affairs, which will be crucial as the company navigates the complex U.S. healthcare market.

Additionally, Michelle Paknad has been appointed to a senior role, further strengthening the company's leadership capabilities. These appointments reflect CMR Surgical's commitment to building a world-class team capable of delivering on the company's ambitious growth plans.

"We're thrilled to welcome these exceptional leaders to our team," said a company spokesperson. "Their expertise will be invaluable as we work to make the Versius Surgical System available to more surgeons and patients around the world."
